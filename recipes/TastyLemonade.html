Tasty Lemonade

This lemonade is good on hot days, and very successful at lemonade stands.

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 4 servings

  • 6 lemons, juiced
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup ice cubes

Directions

  1. In a large pitcher, combine fresh lemon juice and water. Stir in brown sugar until it dissolves. Stir in ice cubes.
